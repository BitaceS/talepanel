## Why this CLA exists

TalePanel is dual-licensed: AGPL-3.0 for public use, plus a commercial license for hosters who cannot accept the AGPL's source-disclosure requirements. For the commercial license to remain enforceable, the copyright holder (Tyraxo) must hold all rights necessary to relicense the combined work. Without a CLA from every contributor, unrelated code under different terms would end up in the repo, and we could no longer issue commercial licenses covering the whole codebase.
