## Why this CLA exists

TalePanel is AGPL-3.0 and stays free for everyone — there is no paid edition and no commercial license to buy. The CLA exists so the maintainer (BitaceS) holds the rights needed to keep the codebase legally coherent: to relicense it under a *future OSI-approved open-source license* if the project ever needs to (for example moving to a later AGPL version), and to defend the license if it is violated. Without a CLA from every contributor, code under conflicting terms accumulates and neither is possible.

To be explicit: this CLA is **not** a path to closing the source. TalePanel is and remains open source.
